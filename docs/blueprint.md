# **App Name**: TurnosYa

## Core Features:

- Appointment Scheduling: Allow customers to schedule appointments by selecting services, preferred professional, and desired time slot.
- Service Selection: Enable customers to choose from a list of available services and view the total cost of the selected services.
- Automated Reminders: Send automated WhatsApp reminders to customers 24 hours and 12 hours before their scheduled appointments.
- Cancellation and Rescheduling: Allow customers to cancel or reschedule appointments up to 12 hours in advance.
- Payment Integration: Integrate with Mercado Pago to allow customers to pay for services online.
- Calendar Sync: Synchronize appointments with Google Calendar and Outlook for professionals and administrators.
- User Roles: Implement different user roles (admin, professional, customer) with specific permissions and access levels.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#673AB7) to convey a sense of luxury and style.
- Background color: Light lavender (#F3E5F5), a desaturated tint of the primary color to give the app a bright and clean feel.
- Accent color: Blue-violet (#9575CD) to create a harmonious yet contrasting emphasis.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, for both headlines and body text, providing a balance of modernity and warmth for clear readability across the interface.
- Use modern, line-style icons related to hair care and beauty services.
- Maintain a clean and modern layout with a clear information hierarchy.
- Use subtle animations and transitions to provide feedback and enhance the user experience.
